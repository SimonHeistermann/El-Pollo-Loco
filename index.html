El Pollo Loco

Controls:

Left Arrow/A: Move Left
Right Arrow/D: Move Right
Up Arrow/SPACE: Jump
E: Throw

Note:

Kill enemies by jumping on them or throwing a bottle at them
Kill the Endboss by throwing multiple bottles at it
Get full health by collecting enough coins
